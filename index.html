See a West End show

Insider Tips for Visiting London

  • Oyster Card: Get an Oyster card or use contactless payment for easy travel on the Tube and buses.
  • Best Time to Visit: Spring and early autumn offer pleasant weather and fewer crowds.
  • Mind the Gap: Always stand on the right on escalators and let passengers off the Tube before boarding.
  • Free Museums: Many of London’s top museums, including the British Museum and the National Gallery, are free to enter.
  • Weather: Pack an umbrella – London weather can be unpredictable!
  • Markets: Explore local markets like Borough Market for food and Camden Market for unique finds.
